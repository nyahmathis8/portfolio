Nyah's background and mission

Hi, I'm Nyah!

Nyah Mathis is a second-year Broadcast & Digital Journalism and Political Science pre-law student at Syracuse University from Indianapolis, Indiana. As both a journalist and student of politics in the current climate, she believes both the law and the media are necessities to hold institutions accountable and give voice to the underrepresented, which is why she plans to use her education in the workforce to better the lives of others.
